Patthai House - 2014

Patthai House is the first building that Pat designed during his last year of high school year at Sprague High School. This project was inspired by traditional Thai architecture and the termite nest. This house was designed to be environmental friendy, but also show the appearance of modern house.

Xenoarch - 2015

This project is Pat's experimental architecture design. The Pat was trying to intergrate biomimetics elements of Beehive into the building structure. This project was designed in Google Sketch up purposeless at the beginning. But at the end, the shape of the building evolve into something that look interesting.

African Museum - 2014

This project is part of Architecture design class at Sprague High School. Pat was inpired by the fascinating form of native African architecture. Pat was trying to mimick the unique abstract curve and circular form of African house and combine it with the modern material to build a museum that represent the dignity and contemporary of African Art.
