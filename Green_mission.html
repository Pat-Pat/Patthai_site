The Green Mission is a game about detoxifying the world with bioremediation (the concept of using organisms to absorb and degrade the toxic chemical).The idea for the game came from Plants vs. Zombies, “but I wanted to change the zombie to something which is bad for the world — like trash, like plastic bags, or something that is like that. Pollution.”This game won “Best Application Award” from CodeDay Corvallis in January,2014.
